DAUGHTER

An English indie folk trio

Here is a timeline of Daughter's evolution:

  • 2010 - The band is formed by North London native Elena Tonra, Swiss guitarist Igor Haefeli and French drummer Remi Aguilella.
  • 2011 - The band releases their debut EP, His Young Heart, recorded in Haefeli's bedsit. Later in the year, they release another EP, The Wild Youth, through artist-led Communion Records.
  • 2012 - The band announce their signing to UK label 4AD and release their first single, "Smother", that year.
  • 2013 - The band release their debut album, If You Leave, which becomes number 16 in the UK charts and wins Independent Album of the Year at the AIM Independent Music Awards in London.
  • 2014 - The band release 4AD Sessions EP.
  • 2015 - The band release the single "Numbers" along with its music video. The song is part of their upcoming album, due to be released in 2016.
  • 2016 - The band release the album Not to Disappear, with the first single "Doing the Right Thing" made available as a music video. They also release a music video for "No Care".
  • 2017 - The band release their third album, Music From Before the Storm, written as the soundtrack of video game Life Is Strange: Before the Storm.
  • 2018 - Elena Tonra announces Ex:Re, her upcoming solo debut album. With its release, Daughter went on hiatus.
  • 2021 - Ex:Re releases Ex:Re with 12 Ensemble.
  • 2023 - The band reunites, releasing their first studio album in seven years, Stereo Mind Game, via Glassnote Records.
